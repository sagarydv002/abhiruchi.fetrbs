About Abhiruchi Society

Abhiruchi Society is a vibrant and creative community of artists and craft enthusiasts at our college. We are dedicated to promoting and nurturing artistic talent and fostering a love for creativity among our members.

Our society organizes a wide range of art and craft events, workshops, and exhibitions throughout the year. These events provide a platform for our members to showcase their talents, learn new skills, and connect with fellow artists.

Over the years, Abhiruchi Society has played a significant role in enhancing the cultural and artistic landscape of our college. We have contributed to various college events, festivals, and community projects, bringing art and creativity to the forefront.

Join us and be a part of the Abhiruchi Society as we continue to inspire and celebrate the beauty of art and craft in our college community.
