Dr. Saloni Srivastava: A Kind-Hearted Convenor Dedicated to Art and Craft


Dr. Saloni Srivastava is a name that resonates with compassion, creativity, and the spirit of community. As the esteemed convenor of the Abhiruchi Society, an organization dedicated to promoting art and craft, she has left an indelible mark on the lives of countless individuals. Dr. Srivastava, a generous and empathetic lady, exemplifies the true essence of a leader who uplifts and inspires others.


While her professional identity rests as an Assistant Professor at Raja Balwant Singh Engineering Technical Campus, teaching engineering mathematics to Bachelor of Technology students, her love for art and craft shines through her commitment to the Abhiruchi Society. This unique blend of technical expertise and artistic passion sets her apart as a multidimensional individual, enriching the lives of those around her.


Dr. Srivastava's genuine affection for art and craft emanates from her heart, and her zeal for the subject is contagious. She understands the transformative power of creative expression and endeavors to provide a platform for individuals to explore and showcase their talents. Under her guidance, the Abhiruchi Society organizes a wide range of events, exhibitions, and workshops, enabling individuals to discover their artistic potential.


Beyond her role as a convenor, Dr. Saloni Srivastava's true essence lies in her unwavering support for others. Her kind-hearted nature shines through her interactions, as she goes above and beyond to ensure that everyone feels valued and encouraged. Driven by a deep sense of empathy, she recognizes the importance of nurturing the well-being of those around her, fostering an inclusive and nurturing environment within the Abhiruchi Society.

Dr. Srivastava's remarkable ability to bring people together is a testament to her exceptional leadership skills. She possesses a unique talent for recognizing and harnessing the strengths of each individual, inspiring them to collaborate and create remarkable works of art. Her approachable demeanor and willingness to listen make her a trusted confidante, offering guidance and support to those in need.
